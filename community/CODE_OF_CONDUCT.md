# 📋 Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

AsyncKeel is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and community members, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and inclusive
- Welcoming differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards others

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Offensive comments related to protected characteristics
- Trolling or deliberate disruption
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Other conduct which could be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. They will investigate violations and take appropriate action.

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience a violation, please report it by:
- Opening a private issue
- Emailing the maintainers
- Reaching out to a community leader

All reports will be reviewed confidentially.
