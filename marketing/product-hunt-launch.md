## Tagline Options (≤60 chars)

1. **FastAPI SaaS boilerplate for indie builders**

## Product Hunt Description (≤260 chars)

AsyncKeel is a FastAPI backend boilerplate for indie SaaS teams. Get JWT auth, RBAC, 2FA, OAuth2 social login, and multi-tenant foundations out of the box so you can ship real products faster with less backend setup.

## First Comment Draft (Founder Story)

Hey Product Hunt 👋

We built AsyncKeel after repeatedly losing weeks rebuilding the same backend pieces for every SaaS idea. As indie builders, we wanted a practical FastAPI foundation with production-ready auth, permissions, and architecture defaults — without enterprise bloat.

This month we added RBAC, 2FA, OAuth2 social login, and early multi-tenant building blocks. Our goal is simple: help small teams ship faster and safer.

We’re building in public, so your feedback directly shapes what we build next. Thanks for checking us out 🙌
